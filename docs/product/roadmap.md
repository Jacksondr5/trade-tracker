## Roadmap Notes

These are not committed design decisions, but they should remain visible.

### Trade-level notes

Current state:

- supported by the model
- not a primary workflow

Future decision:

- either surface them intentionally as part of the trade review workflow
- or remove/de-emphasize them if the product moves toward requiring more explicit trade-plan structure

### Watchlist

Future decision:

- add `Watchlist` as a separate focus layer across campaigns and trade plans
- keep it distinct from lifecycle statuses such as campaign `active` or trade-plan `watching`

### Portfolio relationships

Future decision:

- keep portfolios as trade-linked overlays unless analytics or exposure workflows justify stronger direct relationships elsewhere
